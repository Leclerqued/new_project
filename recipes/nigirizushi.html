Nigirizushi

Nigirizushi consists of an oblong mound of sushi rice that a chef typically presses between the palms of the hands to form an oval-shaped ball and a topping draped over it.

It is usually served with wasabi.

Unagi Nigiri

Ingredients

  • grilled BBQ eel
  • unagi sauce
  • freshly cooked sushi rice
  • toasted sesame seeds
  • nori
Preparation
  1. Use a sharp knife to cut the unagi into equal pieces about 10g each;
  2. Wet your hands and form about 15g of the sushi rice into small oblong shaped pieces;
  3. Place the grilled eel on top of the rice;
  4. Lightly brush the top of the eel with unagi sauce;
  5. Take a strip of nori and wrap it around the middle;
  6. Sprinkle toasted sesame seeds over the top of the eel;
  7. Repeat with the remainder.
